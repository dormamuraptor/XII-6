Overview and History


DJVI is a music producer born on 2nd of July 1992 in southern Finland. He primarily makes electronic music, ranging from techno, house, dubstep, hardstyle, and drum n' bass. His songs are mostly featured in music-based games like Geometry Dash, Dash till Puff!, Dub Dash, Beaty Cubes, etc. Music production isn't his primary job, that is his hobby and passion. Instead, his primary job is an HVAC engineer. He currently lives in Vantaa.

His music passion started from his love towards old-school techno music from the 2000s and artists such as Mangoo, ItaloBrothers, Basshunter, and B0UNC3. He soon decided to get a demo version of FL Studio in 2008 and made small loops. in 2010, He published his first song on Newgrounds titled All I Need.

Soon, he found his first ever fame when six of his songs got featured in a game called Geometry Dash back in 2013 when the developer RobTop was looking for songs for his game and he made a forum for artists to send their songs. The songs in question are in song examples (not all), so please go there if you wanna see it. :P

In 2014, he thought of leaving Newgrounds for YouTube and in 2015 he founded his music label Sound Ward, however, this got shut down in 2017 due to stress and high competition. Currently, he's an independent artist not under any labels.

His first approach in doing something different to his music was in 2022 when he initiated his first story based on his songs titled Scions of the Multiverse, available on his official website. Trivia

  1. DJVI owns a cat and a dog, both named Neko and Jeppe respectively.
  2. Before having his officially set username, DJVI's YouTube username was justfeelthismove before changing to djvitechno and later djviofficial.
  3. There are unpublished DJVI songs that can be found on Web Archive such as EoN, The Beach, and remixes of other songs.
  4. DJVI doesn't like salmiakki (salty licorice) despite the popularity among Finns.
